Buskers Festival

LJUBLJANA

1. - 3. July 2017

Fun for everyone

From July 1st to July 3th, 2016 Ljubljana welcomes the world to our beautiful downtown! Professional street performers from our region as well as from across Europe, Canada, Australia and South America will converge for our fifth Ljubljana International Buskers Festival. Our event is a family oriented, free- to-attend festival presented on mostly outdoor stages. Tips to Buskers hats welcomed and appreciated!

Program

Be sure to visit our many Busk Stops, our International Food Village and take advantage of our various attractions. Click on button to get whole festival program.

Locations

Prešern squaremore_vert
Prešern squareclose

Central square in Ljubljana. It is part of the old town's pedestrian zone and a major meeting point.

Town squaremore_vert
Town squareclose

Major square in Ljubljana. Ljubljana Town Hall is located at the square. In front of Town Hall stands a copy of the Robba Fountain.

Cobblers' bridgemore_vert
Cobblers' bridgeclose

Bridge crossing the river Ljubljanica in Ljubljana.One of the oldest bridges crossing the river in Ljubljana, and dates back to at least the 13th century.

Olg squaremore_vert
Old squareclose

Old Square(Stari trg) is the earliest settled part of medieval Ljubljana. It blends into Upper Square(Gornji trg), which slopes up towards the foot of Ljubljana's castle hill.

Butchers' Bridgemore_vert
Butchers' Bridgeclose

The Butchers' Bridge (Slovene: Mesarski most) is a footbridge crossing the river Ljubljanica in Ljubljana. It connects the Ljubljana Central Market and the Petkovšek Embankment.The largest sculptures on the bridge represent figures from Ancient Greek and Christian/Jewish mythology.

Central Marketmore_vert
Central Marketclose

Ljubljana's Central Market is more than just a place to shop. Traditionally, it has also been a place for the locals to meet and enjoy themselves together.
